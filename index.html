The Story

Independent baker Amelia Heirr has made cake her life's work.

To her, baking is a privilege and she is honored to have such a fulfilling profession that allows her to share in people's most joyous occasions.

Amelia fell in love with baking after visiting her great-grandmother in southern France. After learning about family history and inheriting a few of generational cookbooks, she fled her corporate job in a multimillion-dollar marketing agency to pursue and study the making of pastries.

She now runs an 80-person global team of professional bakers that make cakes and pastries for customers on their most memorable occasions under the brand name "Amelia Heirr".

“We want to create magnificent sweets worthy of the most magical days and everyone deserves to eat cake!” - Amelia
